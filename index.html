Crystal Collector

Instructions

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the winning number and you will lose the game if your total score exceeds the winning number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the game will change the values of of each crystal at random.
